Motherboard

Like the Cases these come in different form factors, and some come with different features like ability to Overclock RAM or CPU or GPU or come with built-in WIFI.

You need a different Motherboard for each CPU

A Ryzen CPU needs a AM4 Socket and an Intel one has many sockets like LGA1200

The Motherboard is most commonly screwed into the back of the Case

Motherboards like the one pictured below are often more expensive as at the top left all of the input and output plugs are in a box.

Whereas cheaper boards are often only secured by the case so are more likely to break or snap.
